Library News

Book Stacking Contest!



- How fast can you build a pile of books four feet high? Try this contest and find out!

- You may work in teams or alone.

- All participants who reach three feet receive a prize and a Green/Gold point.

- Fastest builder who reaches five feet receives 10 Green/Gold points.

Campus Center, Lunchtime, Tues-Wed. Sept. 9-10

Bookfair 2014



Come check out the annual Bookfair on October 23-24 in the Board Room in the Campus Center! Everyone is scheduled to come with their English class, but you may also come during lunch, and after school on Monday. Upper Schoolers are welcome also. Books are provided by the University Bookstore, with help from your suggestions. You may buy books on your student account--no cash or checks. Please check with your parents first, to make sure it's okay for you to buy books! Profits go towards purchasing books for our school in Uruguay.

Bulwer-Lytton Contest



19th century novelist Edward Bulwer-Lytton, famous for such lines as "It was a dark and stormy night..." was such a terrible writer that San Jose University started an annual contest to see who could write opening sentences even worse than his. This year, the Overlake School Library ran its annual Bulwer-Lytton contest, and the results caused a rash of sick days among the hapless faculty who had to vote on the worst of the worst.
